## Why these specific variants

Sprint 1A §3.2 B3 pins `2D 64×64` as the shipping default with `2D 128×128`
and `2D 256×256` as A-B validation variants. The `LDR_LLL1_*` files are
8-bit RGBA with the luminance sample replicated across R=G=B — our loader
(`crates/render/src/noise.rs`) strips these back to grayscale by taking
the R channel.

The experimental `3D 64³` shelf mirrors the 2D choice: 64 slices of the
same `LDR_LLL1_*.png` variant so the existing R-channel loader can be
extended trivially whenever a future sprint needs volumetric blue noise.

The experimental `4D 16⁴` shelf has no PNG form upstream — Calinou's
`16_16_16_16/` directory only ships raw binary. We take `HDR_L.raw`
(single-channel luminance, 32-bit float, 256 KB = 16⁴ × 4 bytes) as the
minimum viable archival. A future consumer should decide whether to
expand to `HDR_LA` / `HDR_RGB` / `HDR_RGBA` based on its actual use.

Full variants (`LDR_RGB1`, `LDR_RGBA`, `HDR_*.png`, additional sizes) are
NOT copied — they are available upstream at the URL above if needed, and
leaving them out keeps the repo from carrying ~20 MB of non-hot-path
assets.
