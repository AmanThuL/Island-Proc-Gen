## Why these specific variants

Sprint 1A §3.2 B3 pins `2D 64×64` as the shipping default with `2D 128×128`
and `2D 256×256` as A-B validation variants. The `LDR_LLL1_*` files are
8-bit RGBA with the luminance sample replicated across R=G=B — our loader
(`crates/render/src/noise.rs`) strips these back to grayscale by taking
the R channel. The experimental `3D 64³` and `4D 16⁴` shelves listed in
the sprint doc are not downloaded here — they are not on the Sprint 1A
hot path and can be added when a future sprint actually consumes them.
